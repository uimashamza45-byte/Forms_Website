Welcome to Greenfield

Greenfield provides a modern education that balances Cambridge O-Level pathways with local college preparation. Our teachers and principal work closely to design personal learning plans, monitor student progress and encourage extracurricular growth.

Quick facts
  • Small class sizes with focused attention
  • Experienced Cambridge-aligned teachers
  • Broad sports program (basketball, football, badminton/racket sports)
  • Active parent-teacher-principal collaboration

About Our School

Greenfield combines the best of structured academics and hands-on learning. The campus is designed with bright classrooms, science labs, a library, and sports courts. We emphasize values, discipline and kindness while encouraging creativity and critical thinking.

Principal & Teacher Cooperation

The principal leads an open, collaborative culture where teachers contribute to curriculum planning, student welfare and extracurricular management. Regular meetings, observations and professional development ensure teachers stay well-equipped, supported and motivated.

Academics

O-Level (Cambridge)

Our Cambridge O-Level program follows internationally recognized standards. Students receive focused support for subject choices, exam preparation and practical coursework. Experienced teachers guide each student toward high achievement and university readiness.

College & Local Programs

For students aiming for local colleges, we provide tailored programs that prepare them for local board exams, college entrance tests and vocational pathways. Counseling helps students choose the right stream.

Academic Support

Remedial classes, tutoring clinics and study workshops are available. Merit awards and scholarship interviews recognize top performers while mentorship programs help students stay on track.

Sports & Physical Education

We believe sports are essential to character building, fitness and teamwork. Our structured sports program includes regular PE lessons, inter-house tournaments, and after-school clubs.

Basketball

Coaching squads for all ages, court access and competitive fixtures.

Football

Training and local league participation with dedicated coach staff.

Badminton & Racket Sports

Indoor courts and rackets provided — shuttle-based drills and tournaments.

Other Games

Athletics, table tennis, and recreational clubs to keep students active.

Admissions & Scholarships

How to Apply

  1. Fill the application form and attach recent report cards.
  2. Attend an entrance assessment and interview (if required).
  3. Successful applicants receive an offer letter and scholarship info (if eligible).

Scholarships

Scholarships are awarded for academic merit, sporting achievements and community leadership. Financial aid is also available for families in need — applications are assessed confidentially.

Note: Scholarships support O-Level and college program students equally.
